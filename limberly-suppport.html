FAQs

How do I start a flow?

Open Flows, choose a routine, then tap Start.

Where can I view pose guidance?

Open Poses to browse pose instructions and cues.

The app looks different on my phone—why?

Layout can vary slightly by screen size and iOS version. If something looks wrong, email support with a screenshot.

Please include:

  • A screenshot or screen recording (optional but helpful)
