1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how [COMPANY_NAME] collects, uses, and shares information when you use Limberly (including our mobile applications and related services). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

Fitness/Wellness disclaimer: The App offers stretching and yoga content for general wellness only and is not a substitute for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a healthcare professional before beginning any exercise program.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Account & profile information

Examples: name or nickname, email address, password (hashed), profile photo (optional), preferences. If you sign in with a third party (e.g., Apple/Google), we receive basic account info per your settings.

2.2 Workout, stretch, and wellness data (optional)

Examples: poses you complete, session history, custom routines, target areas, flexibility goals, notes, streaks. If you enable health integrations (e.g., Apple Health, Google Fit), we may read/write permitted metrics (e.g., workout minutes) strictly to power features you choose. You can disable integrations anytime.

2.3 Device & usage data

Examples: device model, OS version, app version, language, timezone, country/region (inferred), in-app events (e.g., screens viewed), and diagnostic logs—primarily for reliability and analytics.

2.4 Location data (optional)

We do not collect precise geolocation unless you grant permission for features that need it (e.g., localized reminders). Approximate location may be inferred from your IP to localize content (e.g., language).

2.5 Payments & subscriptions

Purchases made through Apple App Store or Google Play are processed by those platforms; we receive limited information (e.g., a transaction ID, product, and status). If we offer direct payments (e.g., via Stripe), we collect the minimum needed for billing and fraud prevention.

2.6 User content & support

Examples: feedback, ratings, emails to support, survey responses, and any files you send us for troubleshooting.

3. How we use information

  • Provide core features: routines, progress tracking, reminders, personalization.
  • Process purchases, manage subscriptions, and communicate about your account.
  • Maintain and improve performance, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • Personalize content (e.g., recommended stretches) and measure effectiveness.
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as described below:

  • Service providers (processors). Vendors that help us run the App under contract (they may only process data per our instructions):
    • Analytics (e.g., Firebase/Google Analytics).
    • Crash reporting (e.g., Sentry, Firebase Crashlytics).
    • Payments & subscriptions (e.g., Apple, Google Play, Stripe, RevenueCat).
    • Infrastructure (e.g., Firebase, AWS, Supabase).
    • Customer support & communications (e.g., Intercom, Zendesk, email providers).
  • Legal, safety & compliance. Where required by law or to protect rights, security, and integrity of our users, the public, or our business.
  • Business transfers. If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

If we ever enable advertising technology, we will update this section and provide necessary notices and choices.

5. Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, while your account is active, or as required by law. If you request deletion, we will delete or de-identify your data unless we must retain certain information (e.g., for fraud prevention, legal obligations, or dispute resolution).

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing, and to data portability. You may also withdraw consent where we rely on consent.

7.1 EEA/UK legal bases

For users in the EEA/UK, we process personal data under these legal bases: (i) to perform our contract with you (provide the App); (ii) our legitimate interests (e.g., improve the App, prevent fraud); (iii) your consent (e.g., health integrations, marketing); and (iv) compliance with legal obligations.

7.2 U.S. state privacy notices

If applicable law in your U.S. state provides additional rights (e.g., to opt out of certain sharing, to limit use of sensitive data), you can exercise those rights by contacting us at apps@paintedsky.club. We do not sell your personal information.

7.3 Do Not Track

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. There is no common standard for DNT, so we do not respond to DNT signals.

To exercise your rights: Email apps@paintedsky.club. We may need to verify your identity.

8. International data transfers

We may transfer, store, and process your information outside your country of residence (e.g., the United States). Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers (such as standard contractual clauses).

9. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the age required by your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

10. How to delete your data

  1. In-app: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account (this permanently removes your account and associated personal data after a short grace period).
  2. Email: Alternatively, send a request to apps@paintedsky.club from the email on your account with the subject “Delete my Limberly account”.

We will confirm deletion unless retention is required by law or legitimate business needs (e.g., fraud prevention). Deletion requests for purchases managed by Apple/Google do not cancel active subscriptions—you must cancel through the app store.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version and change the Last updated date above. If changes are material, we will provide additional notice (e.g., in-app message).
